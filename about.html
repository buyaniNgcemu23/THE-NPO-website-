About OpenHands Foundation

OpenHands Foundation is a South African non-profit organisation. We are here to help people in need and to bring hope to our communities. Our focus is on simple but powerful actions that can make life better for families and young people.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support people through education, food relief, and skills development. We want to give people the tools and chances they need to grow and live a better life.

Our Values

  • Compassion – We care for people with kindness.
  • Honesty – We are open and clear about what we do.
  • Community – We believe in working together.
  • Change – We want to make life better for others.

Communities We Serve

We work in different parts of South Africa. Each area has its own needs, and we try our best to meet those needs through simple but effective programs.

Cape Town

We support families in Cape Town by giving food parcels and running programs for young people. We also create safe spaces where the youth can learn and grow.

Durban

In Durban, we run youth programs, community clean-ups, and health awareness campaigns. We want to give young people hope and purpose through skills training.

Eastern Cape

The Eastern Cape is one of our focus areas for rural development. We help schools with learning materials, support small farmers, and uplift families in rural areas.

Gauteng

In Gauteng, we focus on helping people find work through skills programs and support shelters for people in need. We believe that giving people skills is the best way to help them long-term.
