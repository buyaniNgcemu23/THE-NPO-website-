Our Services

Open Hands Foundation serves communities across South Africa. We focus on food relief, education support, skills training, and practical help.

We travel to different communities — Cape Town, Durban, Eastern Cape, and Gauteng — bringing support directly to the people who need it most. Our teams run workshops, outreach events, and drives that make a real impact.

Food Relief

We provide food parcels, cooked meals, and groceries to families in need. Our volunteers organize food drives to make sure every child and elder in the community has access to nutritious meals.

Education Support

We support schools and learners by providing books, stationery, after-school programs, and mentorship. Our goal is to help children stay in school, gain knowledge, and grow confident for the future.

Skills Training

We run workshops for adults and youth to learn skills like basic computer use, sewing, carpentry, or small business management. These programs help people earn income and improve their livelihoods.

Health & Wellness

Our teams provide basic health checks, wellness workshops, and health awareness campaigns in under-resourced areas. We teach families about hygiene, nutrition, and staying healthy.

Community Outreach

We organize community clean-ups, awareness campaigns, and support local shelters. By working together, we help make neighborhoods safer, cleaner, and more welcoming.
